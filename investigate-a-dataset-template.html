Introduction¶

This project aims to investigate guns data from National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS), a source of FBI. Intended objectives are to find out:

Trend of Firearm checks on yearly basis

State wise Comparison of Gun Permits

Correlation among Gun Permits and Education, Gender, Population
